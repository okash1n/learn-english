Talk about:
- Tell the story of a time an app, site, or system went down — アプリ・サイト・システムが落ちた時の話を時系列で
- How you first noticed and who you told right away — どうやって最初に気づき、誰にすぐ伝えたか
- The steps you took to work around it or fix it — 回避策や直すためにやった手順
- What you and your team changed afterward so it wouldn't happen again — 再発しないように後で変えたこと
