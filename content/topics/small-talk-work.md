Talk about:
- How your morning went before work — 出勤前の朝の様子
- The weather today and how you got to work — 今日の天気と通勤の様子
- What you had for lunch or your coffee break — お昼や休憩で食べたもの・飲んだもの
- A short question to ask your coworker back — 同僚に返す短い質問の例
