title_ja: "朝、猫にごはんをあげる"

level: [1, 2]
---

I have a small cat. Her name is Momo. Every morning, she wakes me up. She sits on my bed and cries. She wants her food. So I can't sleep late anymore.

I get up and go to the kitchen. Momo walks with me. She's always in front of my feet. Sometimes I almost fall down. But I don't get angry. She's just hungry.

I open a can of cat food. Momo hears the sound. She runs to her bowl and waits. I put the food in her bowl. Then she eats fast. She's really happy.

After she eats, I give her fresh water. I clean her bowl too. Momo washes her face with her paw. It's very cute. Then she sits by the window.

Now it's my time. I make some coffee and toast. Momo watches the birds outside. We're both quiet and calm. I love these mornings with her. It's a nice way to start the day.
