I want to tell you about my work laptop. It's pretty old now. Some mornings it's really slow. I press the button, and then I just wait. It's kind of annoying, but I'm used to it.

When it's slow, I open too many things. I have my email, my chat app, and my code editor. Plus a lot of web pages. So the laptop gets tired. I know that's my fault, but I still do it.

Last week a coworker gave me a tip. He said I should close things I don't use. So now I close old web pages. And I turn the laptop off at night. It really does help. I'm glad he told me.

Sometimes I still need to wait for it. When that happens, I get some coffee. Or I write down my plan for the day. It's a nice little break, so I don't mind. But one day I'd love a new, fast laptop.

For now, my old one is okay. It's slow, but it still works. And I've learned to be more careful. Don't you hate it when your computer freezes? I do, but I just smile and wait.
