It's Monday morning, and I'm in a hurry. I need to leave for work soon. But I can't find my keys. This happens to me a lot. I always put them in the same place. Today they're not there.

So I start to look around the house. I check my coat pocket first. They're not in my pocket. Then I look on the kitchen table. I see my phone and a cup, but no keys. I'm getting worried now. The clock says it's already eight.

I try to think. Where did I go last night? I remember I made some tea. I sat on the sofa and watched TV. Maybe I left my keys there. So I walk to the living room and look. I check under the cushions. And there they are! My keys were on the sofa.

I feel so happy. I grab my keys and my bag. I put on my shoes and open the door. It's a nice, sunny day outside. I take a deep breath and smile. I'm not late after all.

Now I have a new plan. I'm going to buy a small bowl. I'll put it by the front door. Every day, I'll drop my keys in it. That way, I'll always know where they are. No more looking every morning. It's a simple idea, but I think it'll help.
