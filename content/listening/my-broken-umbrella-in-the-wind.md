---
id: my-broken-umbrella-in-the-wind
title: "My Broken Umbrella in the Wind"
title_ja: "風で壊れた傘"
domain: daily
level: [3, 4]
format: dialogue
speakers: "Emma, Ken"
---

Emma: Ken, you look really wet. What happened to you?

Ken: My umbrella broke on the way here. The wind was too strong.

Emma: Oh no, that's terrible. Was it a new umbrella?

Ken: Yeah, I bought it last week. I'm so annoyed right now.

Emma: I know that feeling. Cheap umbrellas always break like that.

Ken: It wasn't cheap, actually. That's why I'm upset about it.

Emma: Really? So what did you do with the broken one?

Ken: I threw it in a trash can near the station. It was useless.

Emma: Good idea. You couldn't carry a broken umbrella all day.

Ken: Right. But then I got wet walking the rest of the way.

Emma: You should dry your hair. I have a small towel here.

Ken: Thanks, that's really kind. My head feels cold right now.

Emma: Here you go. Do you want some hot tea too?

Ken: Yes, please. A warm drink sounds perfect after that walk.

Emma: I'll make some green tea. It's my favorite on rainy days.

Ken: Mine too. Thanks for helping me feel better, Emma.

Emma: No problem at all. So will you buy another umbrella?

Ken: I have to. But this time I'll pick a strong one.

Emma: There's a good shop near my house. They sell tough ones.

Ken: Oh, that helps a lot. Can you send me the name?

Emma: Sure, I'll text it to you after work today.

Ken: Great. I don't want to get soaked again tomorrow.

Emma: Is it going to rain tomorrow too? I didn't check.

Ken: The news said maybe. The weather's been strange this month.

Emma: So true. Last week it was hot, and now it's cold.

Ken: I can't decide what clothes to wear anymore.

Emma: Same here. I always bring a jacket just in case.

Ken: That's smart. Maybe I should keep one at the office.

Emma: Good plan. Now drink your tea before it gets cold.

Ken: Okay, okay. Thanks again for everything, Emma. You saved my morning.
