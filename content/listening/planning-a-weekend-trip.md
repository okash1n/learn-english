My friend Kana and I are planning a short trip this weekend. We both work all week, so we really need a break. Last night we sat down and talked about where to go. In the end, we picked a small town near the sea.

We want to leave early on Saturday morning. The train ride takes about two hours, and that's not too long. I already checked the times online, and there's a good one at eight. So we're going to meet at the station at half past seven.

There's a lot to think about before we go. We need to book a room, and I want to find one that's cheap but clean. Kana is looking for nice places to eat near the beach. I'm also making a small list of things to pack, like a towel and my camera.

The weather looks good for now, but I'm still a little worried. Sometimes it rains a lot by the sea in summer. So I'll bring a light jacket, just in case. If it rains, we can still walk around the shops and drink some warm tea.

I can't wait for the weekend to come. It's been a long time since I went somewhere new. I think we'll take lots of photos and try the local food. When I get home, I'll tell you all about it.
