title: "The Meeting Room Was Booked"
title_ja: "会議室が予約されていた"
domain: business
level: [3, 4]
format: dialogue
speakers: "Emma, Ken"
---

Emma: Hi Ken, are you ready for the client meeting?

Ken: Almost. I booked the big room for two o'clock.

Emma: Really? I just walked past it, and it's full.

Ken: What? That can't be right. Let me check the screen.

Emma: Maybe someone booked it before you did.

Ken: You're right. The sales team has it until three.

Emma: Oh no. So where do we put our clients?

Ken: Good question. Let me look for another free room.

Emma: The small one near the kitchen might be open.

Ken: It is, but it only fits four people.

Emma: How many people are coming this afternoon?

Ken: Three from the client side, plus you and me.

Emma: That's five. The small room is too tight.

Ken: I know. I really don't want to feel crowded.

Emma: Could we ask the sales team to move?

Ken: Maybe, but their meeting looks important too.

Emma: True. Let's not bother them if we can help it.

Ken: Wait, the room on the third floor is empty.

Emma: The one with the big window? That's a nice space.

Ken: Yes, and it fits eight people easily.

Emma: Perfect. Can you book it right now?

Ken: Done. I just clicked the button. It's ours.

Emma: Great work. I'll tell the front desk about the change.

Ken: Thanks. And I'll send the clients the new room number.

Emma: Do we need to move any chairs up there?

Ken: No, it already has plenty. We're fine.

Emma: What about the coffee and water?

Ken: I'll carry them up before the clients arrive.

Emma: You're a lifesaver. That was a close one.

Ken: It was. Next time I'll check the booking twice.

Emma: Good idea. These mix-ups happen to everyone sometimes.

Ken: They do. Let's head up and set things up.

Emma: Sure. We still have twenty minutes, so no rush.

Ken: Nice. I feel much calmer now. Thanks for the help.
