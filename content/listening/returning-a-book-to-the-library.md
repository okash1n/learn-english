Yesterday I had to return a book to the library. I borrowed it two weeks ago, and the due date was today. I really liked the story, but I didn't have time to read the last part. So I got up early and put the book in my bag. I didn't want to be late again.

The library is close to my house. I can walk there in about ten minutes. It was a nice morning, and the sky was clear. On the way, I passed a small park with some birds. I felt happy and calm. Sometimes a short walk is the best part of my day.

When I got to the library, it was already open. I said hello to the woman at the desk. She's always friendly and kind. I gave her the book, and she checked it on her computer. Then she smiled and told me everything was fine. I felt glad that I wasn't late this time.

Before I left, I looked at the new books near the door. One book about cooking looked really interesting. I love trying new food, so I borrowed it right away. The woman told me the due date, and I wrote it in my phone. I don't want to forget it again.

On the way home, I thought about the recipes in my new book. Maybe I'll try to make some soup this weekend. I like going to the library because it's quiet and calm. Plus, it's free, and I can find so many new things. I think I'll go back again very soon.
