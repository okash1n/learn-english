title_ja: "夕食の後の皿洗い"

level: [1, 2]
---

Every night after dinner, I wash the dishes. It's a small job, but I like it. My kitchen is warm and quiet. I can hear the water running. It's a nice time to think.

First, I clear the table. I take all the plates and cups to the sink. Then I turn on the warm water. I put a little soap on my sponge. I wash each plate one by one. I start with the cups, and then the big plates last.

Sometimes the pans are hard to clean. The food sticks to them. So I let them sit in water for a few minutes. After that, the food comes off easily. My mom taught me that trick a long time ago.

When everything is clean, I dry the dishes with a towel. Then I put them back in the cupboard. The kitchen looks nice and tidy again. I feel happy when it's all done.

I don't have a machine to wash my dishes. But that's okay. Doing it by hand only takes ten minutes. Plus, it's a good way to end the day. Now I can relax and rest.
