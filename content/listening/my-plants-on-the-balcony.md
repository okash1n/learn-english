I've got a few small plants on my balcony. It's not a big garden, just some pots. I keep them by the window where the sun comes in. Every morning I check them before I leave for work. It's a nice way to start my day.

Last spring, I bought my first plant. It was a small tomato in a green pot. I didn't know much about plants back then. I gave it too much water, and the leaves turned yellow. So I read a little online and tried again.

Now I have three plants. There's the tomato, some basil, and a small flower. The basil smells really good when I touch it. I use the leaves when I cook pasta. It's cheaper than buying it at the shop, and it tastes fresh.

Taking care of plants is not hard, but you can't forget them. On hot days, I water them in the morning and again at night. When it rains, I don't need to do anything. The rain does the work for me, which is nice.

I like watching them grow, slowly, day by day. Last week, my tomato plant made its first small fruit. I was so happy. I think I'll buy one more pot soon. Maybe I'll try something new, like mint or a small pepper.
