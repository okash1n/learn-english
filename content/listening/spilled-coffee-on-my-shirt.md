title: "I Spilled Coffee on My Shirt"
title_ja: "シャツにコーヒーをこぼしちゃった"
domain: daily
level: [1, 2]
format: dialogue
speakers: "Mia, Jack"
---

Mia: Oh no, I just spilled coffee on my shirt.

Jack: Really? Where did it land?

Mia: Right on the front, near my chest.

Jack: That's a big stain. It's so dark.

Mia: I know, and I need to leave soon.

Jack: Do you have another shirt you can wear?

Mia: Yeah, but my clean ones are still wet.

Jack: Oh, from the wash last night?

Mia: Right, I forgot to hang them up.

Jack: That's bad timing. Let me think.

Mia: Maybe I can wash this spot fast.

Jack: Good idea. Use cold water, not hot.

Mia: Why cold? I always use warm water.

Jack: Hot water makes coffee stains stay longer.

Mia: Oh, I didn't know that. Thanks.

Jack: Try a little soap on it too.

Mia: Okay, I'm rubbing it now. It's fading.

Jack: See? It's already looking much better.

Mia: You're right, the stain is almost gone.

Jack: Now you just need to dry it.

Mia: I'll use the hair dryer for a minute.

Jack: Smart. That should work really fast.

Mia: Can you pass me the dryer, please?

Jack: Sure, here you go. Be careful, it's hot.

Mia: Thanks. Okay, the shirt's drying now.

Jack: Good. How much time do you have left?

Mia: About ten minutes before my bus comes.

Jack: That's enough. You'll make it.

Mia: I hope so. My day's off to a rough start.

Jack: Don't worry, these things happen to everyone.

Mia: True. Next time I'll drink my coffee slowly.

Jack: Ha, that's a good plan. Sit down first too.

Mia: Yeah, I was rushing around like always.

Jack: Take a deep breath. You've got this.

Mia: Thanks, Jack. The shirt looks fine now.

Jack: Great. Now go catch that bus!
