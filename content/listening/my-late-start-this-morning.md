title: "My Late Start This Morning"
title_ja: "今朝の遅刻"
domain: business
level: [3, 4]
format: dialogue
speakers: "Emma, Ken"
---

Emma: Good morning, Ken. You look a little tired today.

Ken: Yeah, I overslept. My alarm didn't ring this morning.

Emma: Oh no. So you were late for work?

Ken: About twenty minutes late. I ran to the station.

Emma: Did you miss the morning meeting?

Ken: No, I made it just in time. I was lucky.

Emma: That's good. Did you tell the boss you'd be late?

Ken: Yes, I sent her a quick message on the train.

Emma: Smart move. Was she angry about it?

Ken: No, she said it was fine. It happens sometimes.

Emma: She's kind about these things. What went wrong with the alarm?

Ken: I think my phone was on silent last night.

Emma: Ah, that's an easy mistake to make.

Ken: I know. I turned it on during a movie.

Emma: And then you forgot to turn it back on.

Ken: Exactly. So this morning I heard nothing at all.

Emma: Did you at least have time for breakfast?

Ken: No, I just grabbed a banana and left.

Emma: That's not much. You must be hungry now.

Ken: I am. I'll get a big lunch later.

Emma: Good idea. Maybe buy a second alarm clock too.

Ken: That's a good tip. A real clock, not a phone.

Emma: Right. Then the silent setting won't matter anymore.

Ken: True. I'll stop by the shop after work.

Emma: By the way, did you finish the report from yesterday?

Ken: Almost. I just need to check the numbers again.

Emma: Do you want me to look at it with you?

Ken: That would help a lot. Thank you, Emma.

Emma: No problem. Let's do it after your coffee.

Ken: Perfect. I really need that coffee right now.

Emma: I'll get us both a cup from the machine.

Ken: You're a lifesaver. I'll pay you back tomorrow.

Emma: Don't worry about it. Just fix that alarm tonight.

Ken: I will. No more late starts for me.
