title: "The Printer Is Out of Paper"
title_ja: "プリンターの紙が切れた"
domain: business
level: [1, 2]
format: dialogue
speakers: "Mark, Lucy"
---

Mark: Lucy, can you help me for a second?

Lucy: Sure, Mark. What's the problem?

Mark: The printer won't work. I need these papers now.

Lucy: Let me look. Oh, it's out of paper.

Mark: Really? I didn't see any message.

Lucy: There's a small light here. It's red.

Mark: Oh, I missed that. Where's the paper?

Lucy: It's in the cabinet, next to the door.

Mark: Got it. How much do I put in?

Lucy: Just fill this tray. Don't push too hard.

Mark: Okay. Like this?

Lucy: Yes, that's good. Now close it slowly.

Mark: Done. The red light is gone now.

Lucy: Great. Try printing again.

Mark: It's working! Thanks so much.

Lucy: No problem. It happens all the time.

Mark: Really? I thought I broke it.

Lucy: No, you didn't. It just runs out fast.

Mark: That's good to know. I was worried.

Lucy: By the way, are those the meeting papers?

Mark: Yes, for the two o'clock meeting.

Lucy: Oh, that meeting moved to three.

Mark: Wait, it moved? Nobody told me.

Lucy: The boss sent an email this morning.

Mark: I didn't check my email yet.

Lucy: You should look now, just to be safe.

Mark: Good idea. Let me open it quick.

Lucy: Take your time. You have an extra hour.

Mark: You're right. That's a relief.

Lucy: Do you need help with anything else?

Mark: No, I think I'm okay now.

Lucy: Good. Then I'll get back to my desk.

Mark: Thanks again for the paper trick.

Lucy: Any time. Now you can do it yourself.

Mark: I will. Next time I'll check the light first.

Lucy: That's the way. See you at three.

Mark: See you. I'll bring the papers.
