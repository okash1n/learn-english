Every day I clean my desk before I go home. It only takes a few minutes. But it makes me feel good. So I do it every evening.

First, I put my papers in one pile. I don't like a messy desk. Then I throw away the paper I don't need. I keep the important ones in a folder. That way I can find them fast tomorrow.

Next, I close all my books. I put my pens back in the cup. My coffee cup goes to the kitchen. I don't want it to smell in the morning. It's a small thing, but it helps.

After that, I turn off my computer. I check my chair and push it in. I look at my desk one more time. It's clean and quiet now. I feel ready for the next day.

When my desk is clean, I work better. In the morning I can start right away. I don't waste time looking for things. So I always tidy up before I leave. It's a good habit, and I'm happy I have it.
