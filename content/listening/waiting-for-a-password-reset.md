This morning I couldn't log in to our work system. I typed my password three times. It was wrong every time. I'm sure I knew it, but the computer said no. It's really annoying when that happens.

So I clicked the button that says "Forgot my password." The system said it would send me an email. I waited and waited. The email didn't come. I made some coffee and checked again. Still nothing. I'm not a patient person, so this was hard.

After a few minutes, I asked a coworker for help. She's on the IT team, and she's very kind. She told me to check my spam folder. And there it was! The email was hiding there the whole time. I felt a little silly.

I clicked the link and made a new password. This time I wrote it down in a safe place. I don't want to forget it again. The system let me log in right away. I was so glad.

It's a small thing, but it took thirty minutes. Now I'm behind on my work. Still, I learned something today. Always check the spam folder first. And keep your passwords somewhere safe. That's my little tip for you.
