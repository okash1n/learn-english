id: the-milk-went-bad-this-morning
title: "The Milk Went Bad This Morning"
title_ja: "今朝、牛乳がだめになっていた"
domain: daily
level: [1, 2]
format: dialogue
speakers: "Emma, Tom"
---

Emma: Tom, do we have any milk left?

Tom: Yeah, there's a bottle in the fridge.

Emma: I found it, but it smells really strange.

Tom: Oh no. Let me check the date on it.

Emma: It says last Tuesday. That's five days ago.

Tom: Ugh, that's not good. Please pour it out.

Emma: I already did. The sink smells bad now.

Tom: Sorry about that. I forgot to buy more.

Emma: It's okay. But I wanted milk in my coffee.

Tom: Can you drink it black just for today?

Emma: I can try, but I don't really like it.

Tom: There's some sugar. Maybe that helps a little.

Emma: Good idea. I'll put in two spoons.

Tom: How does it taste now? Any better?

Emma: It's still strong, but I can drink it.

Tom: Great. I'll get fresh milk after work today.

Emma: Thanks. Can you get the big bottle?

Tom: Sure. The big one lasts us all week.

Emma: And maybe some bread too? We're almost out.

Tom: Okay, milk and bread. Anything else you need?

Emma: Some eggs would be nice for the weekend.

Tom: Got it. I'll write a little list.

Emma: That's smart. You always forget one thing.

Tom: Ha, that's true. Last time I forgot butter.

Emma: Yes, and we couldn't make toast that night.

Tom: I remember. You were pretty upset with me.

Emma: Just a little. But it's fine now.

Tom: Let's set a note on the fridge next time.

Emma: Good plan. We can check dates every week.

Tom: Yeah, then nothing will go bad again.

Emma: Perfect. Now finish your breakfast, you're late.

Tom: Oh, you're right! I need to run.

Emma: Have a good day. Don't forget the list!

Tom: I won't. See you tonight with fresh milk.
