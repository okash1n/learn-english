title: "Buying Bread at the Bakery"
title_ja: "パン屋でパンを買う"
domain: daily
level: [1, 2]
---

There's a small bakery near my house. I go there almost every morning. It opens at seven, and I like to get there early. The bread smells so good. I can smell it from outside the door.

The woman at the shop is very kind. She always says good morning to me. I usually buy two things. I get one loaf of bread and one sweet roll. The bread is warm and soft. It's still hot from the oven.

One morning, I was in a hurry. I only had a little money with me. I wanted three rolls, but I couldn't buy them all. So I bought just one. The woman smiled and gave me a small cookie too. She said it was a gift. I was really happy.

Now I go there every day. It's not just about the bread. I like to say hello to her. It's a nice way to start my day. A warm roll and a kind word make me feel good.

Sometimes I sit on a bench outside. I eat my roll and watch the street. People walk by with their coffee. Birds come close and wait for crumbs. It's quiet and calm. I don't want to rush. These small moments are my favorite part of the day.
