Last week I got my new work badge. It's a small card with my photo on it. I need it to open the front door. Without it, I can't get inside the office. So I try not to lose it.

On my first day, my boss gave me the badge. She showed me how to use it. You just hold the card near the door. Then the light turns green. And the door opens with a little click. It's easy and quick.

But this morning I couldn't find it. I looked in my bag, but it wasn't there. My heart was beating fast. I was worried I'd be late. Then I found it in my coat pocket. What a relief!

Now I keep my badge in the same place. I put it in the front pocket of my bag. That way I always know where it is. I don't want to search for it again. A small habit like this really helps.

At work, we use the badge for many things. I use it to buy coffee. I also use it to print my papers. It's a very useful little card. I'm glad I have it now.
