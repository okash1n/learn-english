So this morning it was raining hard, and I grabbed my favorite umbrella before I left. It's a nice blue one my sister gave me last year. I really like it, so I try to keep it safe. But guess what? I left it on the train.

I didn't notice until I got to the office. My hands felt empty, and then it hit me. I probably put it on the seat next to me and just forgot. I felt a little silly, but it happens to everyone, right?

At lunch I decided to do something about it. I looked up the train company's lost and found online. There was a phone number, so I called them. A kind woman answered and asked me a few questions. She wanted the time, the train line, and the color of the umbrella.

She said they get so many umbrellas every rainy day. That made me laugh a bit. She told me to check back in two or three days. If someone hands it in, they'll keep it for me. So now I just have to wait and hope.

I'm not sure I'll get it back, but I feel better because I tried. Next time I'll be more careful when I stand up to leave. Maybe I'll tie a little ribbon on the handle so I remember. For now, I'm using a cheap umbrella from the convenience store.
