title_ja: "通話の前にヘッドセットが壊れた"
domain: it
level: [1, 2]
format: dialogue
speakers: "Ken, Emma"
---

Ken: Emma, can you hear me? My headset's acting up.

Emma: No, I can't hear you well. It's really quiet.

Ken: That's bad. We've got the team call soon.

Emma: When does it start? Do we have time?

Ken: In ten minutes. I'm a bit worried now.

Emma: Okay. Let's check your headset first. Is it plugged in?

Ken: Yeah, it's plugged in. But the light isn't on.

Emma: Try a different port on your laptop.

Ken: Good idea. Let me move the cable over.

Emma: Any change? Is the light on now?

Ken: No, still nothing. This headset's pretty old.

Emma: Maybe the cable's broken inside. That happens a lot.

Ken: You're probably right. I've used it for years.

Emma: Don't worry. You can just use your earphones.

Ken: Oh, the ones with my phone? Good thinking.

Emma: Yes. They usually work fine for calls.

Ken: Let me grab them from my bag. One second.

Emma: Take your time. We've still got a few minutes.

Ken: Okay, I've plugged them in. Can you hear me?

Emma: Yes! That's much clearer now. Great.

Ken: What a relief. I thought I'd miss the call.

Emma: You wouldn't miss it. We'd just fix it together.

Ken: Thanks, Emma. You're always so calm about this.

Emma: It's no big deal. Small tech problems happen daily.

Ken: True. I should buy a new headset soon.

Emma: You can ask the office for one. It's free.

Ken: Really? I didn't know that. How do I ask?

Emma: Just send a message to the IT team.

Ken: Perfect. I'll do that after the meeting.

Emma: Good. Now let's join before we're late.

Ken: Right. I'm opening the call link now.

Emma: Great. And test your mic once more inside.

Ken: Will do. Thanks again for the quick help.

Emma: Anytime, Ken. See you in the call.
