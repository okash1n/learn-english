I started a new job last week. On my first day, they gave me a work email. I was a little nervous, but I'm happy about it. It feels like I'm really part of the team now.

At first, I couldn't log in. I typed my password, but it didn't work. It's annoying when that happens, right? So I asked a coworker, and she helped me. She was really kind. It turned out I made a small typo.

Now I get lots of emails every day. Some are about meetings. Some are about new tasks. I try to read them slowly. My English isn't perfect yet, so I use a dictionary sometimes. That's okay. I'm learning more each day.

One thing I like is the folders. I can put emails into groups. So I made a folder for my team, and one for my boss. It keeps things clean. I don't like a messy inbox. It makes me feel calm when it's tidy.

In the morning, I open my email first. I check what's new, and I make a small plan. It's a nice way to start the day. I'm still slow, but I'm getting better. I think I'll be fine.
