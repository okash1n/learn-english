I had a small problem at work today. I got a new email from a client. It asked about our new plan. But I wasn't sure about the price. So I needed to ask my boss.

My boss is often very busy. She has a lot of meetings. I didn't want to bother her. But I needed the right answer. It's better to ask than to guess.

So I walked to her desk. She was reading something on her screen. I said, "Excuse me, do you have a minute?" She smiled and said yes. That made me feel calm.

I asked her about the price. She told me it quickly. It only took two minutes. I said thank you and went back to my desk. Then I wrote a good reply to the client.

I learned something today. It's okay to ask questions. My boss likes clear questions. And a short question saves time for everyone. Next time, I won't feel so nervous.
