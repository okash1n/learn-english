title: "Covering for a Sick Coworker"
title_ja: "体調不良の同僚の代わりを引き受ける"
domain: business
level: [5, 6]
format: dialogue
speakers: "Emma, Ken"
---

Emma: Ken, do you have a minute? I need a small favor.

Ken: Sure, what's going on?

Emma: Ryan called in sick this morning. He sounds pretty bad.

Ken: Oh no. Is it the flu again?

Emma: I think so. He said he'll be out for two days.

Ken: That's rough. So who's covering his tasks?

Emma: That's why I'm asking. Could you handle his client emails?

Ken: I can try. But I don't know his accounts well.

Emma: Don't worry. He left detailed notes in the shared folder.

Ken: Okay, that helps. Are any of them urgent?

Emma: One is. The Tanaka order needs a reply by noon.

Ken: Noon? That's tight. What do they need exactly?

Emma: They asked about the delivery date. It got pushed back a week.

Ken: Got it. Should I tell them the new date directly?

Emma: Yes, please. And apologize for the delay too.

Ken: Of course. Do we have the exact shipping day?

Emma: It's the fifteenth. The warehouse confirmed it yesterday.

Ken: Perfect. I'll draft the email and send it to you first.

Emma: That'd be great. I'll check it before it goes out.

Ken: Sounds good. What about his afternoon meeting?

Emma: Oh, right. I completely forgot about that one.

Ken: It's the weekly review with the design team, isn't it?

Emma: Yes. Can you join and take some notes?

Ken: Sure, but my schedule's a bit full today.

Emma: I know it's a lot. I really appreciate your help.

Ken: No problem. We all need backup sometimes.

Emma: Exactly. I'll take his other calls myself.

Ken: Good plan. Should I message Ryan to check in?

Emma: Maybe later. Let him rest for now.

Ken: Fair enough. I'll start on that Tanaka email right away.

Emma: Thank you so much, Ken. You're a lifesaver.

Ken: Anytime. Just buy me a coffee later, okay?

Emma: Deal. I'll grab one for you after lunch.

Ken: Great. I'll send you that draft in ten minutes.

Emma: Perfect. I'll be at my desk waiting.
