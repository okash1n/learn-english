---
id: the-parcel-left-next-door
title: "The Parcel Left Next Door"
title_ja: "隣に置かれた荷物"
domain: daily
level: [5, 6]
format: dialogue
speakers: "Emma, Ken"
---

Emma: Hey Ken, did a parcel arrive for me today?

Ken: I'm not sure. I didn't see anything by the door.

Emma: That's strange. The app says it was delivered at noon.

Ken: Really? Maybe the driver left it somewhere else.

Emma: I hope not. It's a birthday gift for my sister.

Ken: Oh, that's important. When's her birthday?

Emma: This Saturday, so I can't afford to lose it.

Ken: Let me check the photo the driver usually takes.

Emma: Good idea. Sometimes they snap a picture of the spot.

Ken: Here it is. That doesn't look like our door, though.

Emma: You're right. That's a blue mat, ours is grey.

Ken: I think that's the flat next door, number twelve.

Emma: Of course. The driver mixed up the numbers again.

Ken: It happens a lot around here, honestly.

Emma: Should we go and knock on their door now?

Ken: Sure, but I've never actually met them before.

Emma: Me neither. I only see them in the hallway sometimes.

Ken: Well, let's just be friendly and explain the mix-up.

Emma: Okay. I'll bring the photo to show them.

Ken: Wait, someone's coming out of number twelve right now.

Emma: Perfect timing. Let's ask before they leave.

Ken: Excuse me, sorry to bother you so late.

Emma: A parcel of mine may have gone to your door.

Ken: The driver took a photo, and it looks like yours.

Emma: It's a small box with a red label on top.

Ken: Did you happen to see anything like that?

Emma: They're nodding. I think they found it.

Ken: Oh good, they're going back inside to grab it.

Emma: What a relief. I was really starting to worry.

Ken: See? It wasn't lost after all.

Emma: Here it comes, and the box looks fine.

Ken: Great. Now your sister's gift is safe.

Emma: Thank you so much. I owe you a coffee.

Ken: No problem. Let's report the wrong address later.

Emma: Agreed. Maybe next time they'll get it right.
