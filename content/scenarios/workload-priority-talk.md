Roleplay setup:
- You are meeting your manager one-on-one because you have too many tasks and cannot finish them all this week.
- The AI plays your manager, who is supportive but needs the important work done on time.
- Goal: Clearly explain your situation, propose which tasks to prioritize or hand off, and agree on a realistic plan.
> Thanks for making time for me today.
> I wanted to talk about my workload this week.
> Honestly, I'm a bit stretched right now and could use your help prioritizing.
