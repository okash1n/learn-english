Roleplay setup:
- You are taking a short coffee break and start talking with a coworker in the break room.
- The AI plays a coworker who is also on a break and happy to chat.
- Goal: Practice friendly small talk and asking simple questions about someone's day and work.
> Hi! Do you have a minute to talk?
> Is this seat free?
> How is your day going so far?
