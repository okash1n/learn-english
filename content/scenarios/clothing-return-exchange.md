Roleplay setup:
- You bought a jacket online but it's too tight, so you visit the store to return or exchange it
- The AI plays the store's customer service clerk handling returns
- Goal: Explain the problem, decide between a refund or a size exchange, and confirm the next steps
> Hi, I'd like to return this jacket I bought last week.
> It fit fine in the photos, but the sleeves are way too tight on me.
> Could I exchange it for a larger size instead of getting my money back?
