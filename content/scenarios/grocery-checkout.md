Roleplay setup:
- You are buying a few things at a small grocery store and paying at the checkout.
- The AI plays the friendly cashier at the checkout.
- Goal: Greet the cashier, answer simple questions, and pay for your items.
> Hi, I would like to buy these, please.
> Here are my things.
> Can I pay with a card?
