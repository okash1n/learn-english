Roleplay setup:
- You want to take next Friday off and you ask your manager for it.
- The AI plays your manager, who checks the team's schedule.
- Goal: Explain why you need the day, answer questions, and agree on a plan.
> Do you have a minute to talk about my schedule?
> I would like to take next Friday off.
> Can I ask you about using a day of my vacation?
