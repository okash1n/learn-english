Roleplay setup:
- You are a developer who was assigned to fix a feature in an old part of the codebase you don't understand.
- The AI plays a senior engineer who wrote that code years ago and is walking you through it.
- Goal: Ask clear questions about how the module works, take notes, and confirm your understanding before you start editing.
> Thanks for making time to walk me through this old module.
> Could you explain what this function is actually responsible for?
> So just to confirm, this part handles the payment retries, right?
