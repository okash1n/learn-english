Roleplay setup:
- You are at the library and want to borrow two books.
- The AI plays a friendly library worker at the front desk.
- Goal: Borrow the books and ask when to return them.
> Hi, I would like to borrow these two books.
> Excuse me, can I use my card here?
> When do I need to bring the books back?
