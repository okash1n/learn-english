Roleplay setup:
- You arrive at a hotel and check in for your room.
- The AI plays the hotel front desk worker.
- Goal: Give your name, get your room key, and ask about breakfast time.
> Hello, I have a room for tonight.
> My name is Sato. I booked a room.
> Can I check in now, please?
