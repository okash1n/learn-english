Roleplay setup:
- You are an office worker calling the IT helpdesk because your laptop is not working well.
- The AI plays an IT helpdesk staff member who asks questions and gives simple steps to fix the problem.
- Goal: Explain the laptop problem clearly and follow the helpdesk staff's steps to try to fix it.
> Hi, my laptop is not turning on.
> Hello, I have a problem with my computer.
> Sorry to bother you, but my laptop is very slow today.
