Roleplay setup:
- You are a developer in your team's end-of-sprint retrospective, sharing what went well and what slowed you down.
- The AI plays your scrum master running the retro and asking follow-up questions.
- Goal: Explain one success and one problem clearly, then suggest a concrete improvement for next sprint.
> Thanks for setting this up, should I start with what went well?
> One thing that really slowed us down this sprint was the flaky tests.
> I have an idea that might help us next time.
