Roleplay setup:
- You are calling a restaurant to order some food for pickup.
- The AI plays the restaurant staff member who takes your order on the phone.
- Goal: Order at least two items, give your name, and check when the food will be ready.
> Hi, I'd like to order some food for pickup, please.
> Can I ask what time you close tonight?
> I'd like one large pizza and a green salad.
