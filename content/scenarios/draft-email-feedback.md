Roleplay setup:
- You wrote an important email to a client and want a coworker to review the wording before you send it.
- The AI plays a helpful colleague sitting next to you who reads the draft and gives honest suggestions.
- Goal: Explain what the email is for, ask specific questions about tone and clarity, and decide what to change.
> Do you have a minute to look over an email before I send it?
> I'm not sure this sounds polite enough to the client.
> Could you tell me if the last paragraph is clear?
