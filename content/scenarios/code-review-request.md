Roleplay setup:
- You are a developer who just finished a small change and want a coworker to check your code before you merge it.
- The AI plays a friendly senior teammate who has time to look at your code.
- Goal: Explain what you changed, why, and politely ask for a review.
> Hi, do you have a few minutes to look at my code?
> I finished the login fix and I'd like a review.
> Can you check my changes before I merge them?
