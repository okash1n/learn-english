Roleplay setup:
- You are at a hair salon and want to explain the haircut and length you have in mind.
- The AI plays your hairstylist asking about your preferences and offering suggestions.
- Goal: describe the style you want clearly, react to the stylist's advice, and agree on a plan.
> Hi, I'd like to get my hair cut a bit shorter today.
> Could you take a little off the sides but leave the top longer?
> I'm not sure what suits me, so I'd love your advice.
