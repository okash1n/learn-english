Roleplay setup:
- You need a meeting room for tomorrow afternoon and ask a coworker who manages the schedule.
- The AI plays a coworker who keeps the room booking list.
- Goal: Say when and how long you need the room, and agree on a time that is free.
> Hi, can I book a meeting room for tomorrow?
> I need a room for about one hour in the afternoon.
> Is the small room free after lunch?
