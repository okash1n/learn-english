Roleplay setup:
- You are a tenant calling your landlord because the kitchen faucet has been dripping for a few days.
- The AI plays your landlord, who wants details before sending someone to fix it.
- Goal: Clearly describe the problem and agree on a day and time for the repair.
> Hi, this is the tenant in apartment 3B — do you have a minute?
> I'm calling because my kitchen faucet won't stop dripping.
> Sorry to bother you, but I think something needs to be fixed in my place.
