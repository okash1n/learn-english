Roleplay setup:
- You are dropping off a shirt and a coat at a dry cleaning shop.
- The AI plays the shop worker at the counter.
- Goal: Explain what needs cleaning, ask about the price and pickup day, and get a receipt.
> Hi, I have two things I want to get cleaned.
> There is a stain on this shirt. Can you get it out?
> When can I pick these up?
