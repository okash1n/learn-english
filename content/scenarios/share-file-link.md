Roleplay setup:
- You need a document for your work and ask a coworker to send you the link.
- The AI plays a friendly coworker who has the file.
- Goal: Ask for the file, say which one you need, and thank the coworker.
> Hi, do you have a minute to help me?
> I need the report file. Can you send me the link?
> Which folder is the file in?
