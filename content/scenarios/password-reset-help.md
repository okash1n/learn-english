Roleplay setup:
- You work on the IT support team and help a coworker who cannot log in to their computer.
- The AI plays the coworker who forgot their password and needs help.
- Goal: Understand the problem and guide the coworker through resetting their password step by step.
> Hi, I heard you can't log in. What happens when you try?
> Let's fix this. Can you tell me the error you see?
> No problem, I can help you reset your password.
