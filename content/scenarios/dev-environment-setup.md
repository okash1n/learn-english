Roleplay setup:
- You are a developer helping a new teammate get the project running on their laptop for the first time.
- The AI plays the new teammate, who follows your steps but keeps hitting small errors and asking questions.
- Goal: Walk them through cloning the repo, installing dependencies, and starting the app locally until it runs.
> Hi, let's get your environment set up so you can run the app.
> First, have you cloned the repository to your machine yet?
> Okay, tell me exactly what error message you're seeing on the screen.
