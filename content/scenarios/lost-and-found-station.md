Roleplay setup:
- You left your bag on the train and go to the station office to ask about it.
- The AI plays the station staff member at the lost and found desk.
- Goal: Describe your bag clearly and find out if it was turned in.
> Excuse me, I think I left my bag on the train.
> Hi, I'm looking for a bag I lost this morning.
> Can you help me? I forgot something on the train.
