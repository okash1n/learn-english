Roleplay setup:
- You are meeting your manager one-on-one to ask for a salary raise, backed by your recent contributions.
- The AI plays your manager, who is supportive but has to consider the budget and the team's fairness.
- Goal: Make a clear, evidence-based case for a raise and reach a concrete next step, even if the answer isn't immediate.
> Thanks for making time today. I'd like to talk about my compensation.
> Over the past year I've taken on a lot more, and I think it's worth revisiting my salary.
> Before we start, is now still a good time to discuss my pay?
