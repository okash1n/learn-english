Roleplay setup:
- You need next Friday off and want a coworker to trade shifts with you.
- The AI plays a coworker who is not sure they can take your shift.
- Goal: Explain why you need the day off and agree on a fair swap.
> Hi, do you have a minute to talk about the schedule?
> I need next Friday off, and I was hoping we could swap shifts.
> Could you take my Friday shift if I take one of yours?
