Roleplay setup:
- You found a problem in the app and you tell a developer on your team what happened.
- The AI plays the developer who wants to fix the bug and asks you questions.
- Goal: Explain the bug clearly, share the steps to make it happen, and agree on what comes next.
> Hi, I think I found a bug in the app.
> Do you have a minute to hear about a problem I saw?
> When I click the save button, nothing happens.
