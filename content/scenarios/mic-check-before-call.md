Roleplay setup:
- You join an online meeting a few minutes early to check your microphone and camera with a coworker.
- The AI plays a coworker who is already in the meeting and helps you test your sound and video.
- Goal: Ask if your voice is clear, fix a small problem, and get ready before the meeting starts.
> Hi, can you hear me okay?
> Sorry, I want to test my microphone before we start.
> Is my camera working on your side?
