Roleplay setup:
- You are going to be out of the office tomorrow and you are handing over your open tasks to a coworker.
- The AI plays the coworker who will cover your work while you are away.
- Goal: Explain your open tasks clearly and make sure your coworker knows what to do.
> Thanks for covering for me tomorrow.
> I want to walk you through a few things before I leave.
> There are two tasks I need your help with while I'm out.
