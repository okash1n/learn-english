Roleplay setup:
- You cannot find more paper for the printer and ask a coworker for help.
- The AI plays a friendly coworker who knows where things are kept.
- Goal: Ask where the paper is, and thank the coworker for the help.
> Excuse me, do you have a minute?
> The printer is out of paper. Where can I find more?
> Sorry to bother you, but I need some help.
